designed by Wang Zhi-Hong, published by Christopher Adams and Sophie Chiang, and
released under a Creative Commons license.

Rheingold, Lawrence Liang, Cory Doctorow, Yochai Benkler, Isaac Mao, and Marko
Ahtisaari.
