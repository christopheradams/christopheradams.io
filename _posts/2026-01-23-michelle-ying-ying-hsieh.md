Michelle Ying-Ying Hsieh inaugurated the Taipei edition of [PAVILION] at the
Grand Courtyard in Da'an, then threw an after party at Pure Love Diner Bar in
Gongguan. The night's theme: *nostalgia*.

[PAVILION] is a new alternative art fair founded by Willem Molesworth and
Ysabelle Cheung.
